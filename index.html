About Us

Bridging the Gap for Elderly Care

ElderBridge Support Network is a community-driven initiative dedicated to improving the lives of elderly individuals who often find themselves isolated or without adequate support systems.

Our organization recognizes the growing need for compassionate care and companionship for seniors, particularly those living alone or with limited family or financial resources.

Through our Starter Plan, we aim to establish a foundation of support that can grow and adapt to meet the diverse needs of our elderly community members.

Our Mission

Our Mission

To provide companionship, care, and dignity to elderly people in our community by bridging the gap between their needs and the support they deserve.

Our Activities

Community Outreach

Identify vulnerable elders through churches, community leaders, and word-of-mouth referrals to ensure we reach those most in need.

Home Visits

Conduct regular home visits to provide companionship, assess needs, and offer assistance with daily tasks when appropriate.

Care Kits

Prepare and distribute small care kits containing fruit, snacks, hygiene products, and other essentials to our elderly friends.

Story Sharing

Collect and share stories from our elderly participants to inspire community respect and involvement in elder care.

Progress Tracking

Maintain detailed visit logs to track elder well-being, identify trends, and measure the impact of our program.

3-Month Timeline

Month 1: Foundation

  • Recruit and train initial volunteers
  • Identify elders in need through community networks
  • Develop visit schedules and routes
  • Create basic care package templates

Month 2: Implementation

  • Begin weekly visits to established participants
  • Deliver first round of care kits
  • Establish visit log system
  • Gather initial feedback and stories

Month 3: Expansion

  • Assess program impact and participant well-being
  • Expand to additional elders as capacity allows
  • Share stories to inspire community involvement
  • Plan for future growth and sustainability
